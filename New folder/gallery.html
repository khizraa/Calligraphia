About The ARTIST

khizra faisal is a painter based in Karachi, Pakistan. After completing her degree in Visual Arts from National College of Arts, she joined the University of Arts London where she further studied different techniques of painting. Khizra aims to achieve a balance between color luminosity but also to break contrast between harmony and chaos
